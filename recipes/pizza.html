Pizza

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Dried Oregnao
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Canned Crushed Tomatoes
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Fresh Basil
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Knead the dough
  3. Preheat Oven and Prepare the Pizza
  4. Add Mozarella Cheese
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 4-6 minutes
  6. Take out and enjoy while hot!
